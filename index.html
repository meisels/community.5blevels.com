Introducing... Community 5b!!!


Community 5b is a place where you can upload your levels or levelpacks for other people to view and play. You will need this app to play the levels (unless you want to manually download them).


If you want to submit a level and/or a levelpack, email it to me at ari@5blevels.com.

For security reasons, I cannot just allow mods to be on my site. Some mods, like the ones made by me or Poley Magik are allowed because I know they're not viruses.


One rule...

  • Make your level(s) kid friendly. That means no inappropriate language (censoring is allowed).

    • The current version is Pre-Alpha (v0.0.2)
